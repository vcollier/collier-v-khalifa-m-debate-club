Hip Hop, Yay or Nay?

Hip Hop Rocks!

Everyone like different music genres for different reasons. Generally, we tend to lean towards
the type of music that makes us feel strong emotions or music we just want to jam out to. Hip
hop fills both of these voids. Strong poetry and real-life experiences draws the listener in, relating
to the lyrics. However, the beautiful lyrics aren't the only thing that makes hip hop the best genre.
With these lyrics come catchy beats and music composition that makes you want to dance. To
support this evidence, we need an example. The example aritist we will discuss today is the one
and only Drake. Drake is a Canadian Hip hop artist born and raised in Toronto. Almost all of his
songs talk about his past, and his present and how they effect one another. Drake is a popular artist
in the United States, but an even more popular artist here in Canada. A lot of his songs contain
information and stories from Canada, drawing in the listeners who can relate. Most recently, Drake
came out with his music video for 'I'm Upset', following his around 'Degrassi High' form the
popular Canadian TV show Degrassi. This brought a lot of attention from old fans of the show. In
cuonclusion, Hip hop reaches into the hearts and souls of its listeners and pulls at the heart strings.
Hip hop rocks!
